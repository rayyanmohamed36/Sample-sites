From Flame to Table

Ember & Sage is a celebration of the balance between fire and delicacy. Our open kitchen anchors the dining room, while reclaimed oak and molten bronze details set an intimate glow. Every course echoes our commitment to biodynamic sourcing and zero-waste cooking.

Chef Lena partners with nearby growers and fishermen, crafting nightly menus that highlight the peak of each harvest. Step inside, and savor the warmth.
